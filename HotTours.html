  • Japan (Japanese: 日本 Nihon, Nippon, literally 'the place where the sun rises') is an island nation in East Asia. Situated in the Pacific Ocean, it lies to the east of the Sea of Japan, China, North and South Korea, and Russia. It spans from the Sea of Okhotsk in the north to the East China Sea and Taiwan in the south. Its official political name is the 'Land of the Rising Sun.

  • Egypt - officially known as the Arab Republic of Egypt [Gumhūrīyat Miṣr al-ʿArabīyah], is a transcontinental country situated in North Africa and the Middle East (Sinai Peninsula). It shares borders with Israel and the partially recognized State of Palestine to the northeast, Sudan to the south, and Libya to the west. To the north, its territory is bordered by the Mediterranean Sea, and to the east by the Red Sea, which are connected by the man-made Suez Canal.

  • Ireland - to the north, it borders Northern Ireland (part of the United Kingdom). Ireland has a moderate maritime climate. Along the western coast of the island runs the warm North Atlantic Current, which, along with southwest winds from the Atlantic Ocean, brings warm and humid air masses. Winters are relatively mild, and summers are cool. The warmest month is July, with average temperatures ranging from 18 to 20 degrees Celsius. The coldest month is January, with temperatures dropping to 7-9 degrees Celsius.
